7.1

연습문제 3.7의 자료로부터 다음을 구하여라.

1) $X'X$

$X'X$ = $ \begin{bmatrix} n & \sum_{}^{}x_i \\\\ \sum_{}^{}x_i & \sum_{}^{}{x_i}^2 \end{bmatrix}$

n , $\sum_{}^{}x_i$ , $\sum_{}^{}{x_i}^2$

n <- length(x)
sx <- sum(x)
sxs <- sum(x^2)
c(n,sx,sxs)

    $\to X'X = \begin{bmatrix} 9 & 37.2 \\\\ 37.2 & 155.48 \end{bmatrix}$

    2) $(X'X)^{-1}$

    $(X'X)^{-1}$ = $ \frac{1}{n\sum_{}^{}{x_i}^2 - (\sum_{}^{}x_i)^2} $ $\begin{bmatrix} \sum_{}^{}{x_i}^2 & -\sum_{}^{}x_i \\\\ -\sum_{}^{}x_i & n \end{bmatrix}$

    - $ \frac{1}{n\sum_{}^{}{x_i}^2 - (\sum_{}^{}x_i)^2} $ 계산

    M <- 1/(n*sxs-(sx)^2)

    $ \to (X'X)^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 10.04 & -2.403 \\\\ -2.403 & 0.581 \end{bmatrix} $

    3) $X'Y$

    $X'Y$ = $\begin{bmatrix} \sum_{}^{}y_i \\\\ \sum_{}^{}x_iy_i \end{bmatrix} $

    $ \sum_{}^{}y_i , \sum_{}^{}x_iy_i $

    sy <- sum(y)
sxy <- sum(x*y)

    $\to X'Y$ = $\begin{bmatrix} 23.3 \\\\ 97.24 \end{bmatrix} $

    4) $(X'X)^{-1}X'Y$

    $(X'X)^{-1}X'Y$ = $\begin{bmatrix} \frac{(sxs*sy -sx*sxy)}{n*sxs-sx^2}\\\\ \frac{n*sxy - sx*sy}{n*sxs - sx^2} \end{bmatrix} $
